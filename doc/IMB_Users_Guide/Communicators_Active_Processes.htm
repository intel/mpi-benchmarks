Communicators, Active Processes

Communicator management is repeated in every "select MY_COMM" step. If it exists, the previous communicator is freed. When you run Q<=P processes, the first Q ranks of MPI_COMM_WORLD are put into one group, and the remaining P-Q get MPI_COMM_NULL.

The group of MY_COMM calls the active processes group.
